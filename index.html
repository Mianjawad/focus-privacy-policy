Privacy Policy for Focus - App Usage Limiter

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Focus is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is stored locally on your device and how third-party advertising services may collect information when you use our mobile application.

Our Privacy Commitment

Focus does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers:

  • We do not have any backend servers
  • We do not collect your personal information
  • We do not upload any data to the cloud
  • We do not track your activity across apps
  • We do not share your data with third parties (except advertising partners as described below)
  • All app usage data stays on your device only

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it:

1. App Usage Data

  • What: Time spent in each application, app launch counts, and usage patterns
  • How: Collected through Android's Usage Stats API
  • Purpose: To track and enforce the app time limits you set
  • Storage: Local database on your device only

2. App Settings and Preferences

  • What: Your configured time limits, PIN codes, emergency access duration, and network blocking preferences
  • How: When you configure settings in the app
  • Purpose: To provide the app limiting functionality
  • Storage: Encrypted local storage using Android's DataStore

3. Network Control Settings

  • What: WiFi, mobile data, and background refresh preferences for each app
  • How: When you configure network controls
  • Purpose: To block network access for restricted apps
  • Storage: Local database on your device

Third-Party Advertising

To support the development and maintenance of Focus, we display advertisements through Google AdMob. These advertising services may collect and process certain information:

Information Collected by Google AdMob

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Ad interaction data: Which ads you view or click on
  • General location: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS location)
  • App usage: Time spent in the app, general usage patterns

Purpose of Ad Data Collection

  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To measure ad performance
  • To prevent fraud and abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

Ad Personalization

You can control ad personalization:

  • Visit Google Ad Settings: https://adssettings.google.com
  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads)
  • You will still see ads, but they may be less relevant to you

Third-Party Ad Partners

We use the following advertising services:

  • Google AdMob - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

These services have their own privacy policies and data practices that are independent of Focus.

Data Security

  • All locally stored data is protected by Android's secure application sandbox
  • Data is only accessible by the Focus app
  • Your device's security measures (lock screen, encryption) protect your data
  • We do not transmit your usage data, settings, or any personal information to our servers

Permissions We Request

Usage Access Permission

  • Purpose: To monitor app usage time on your device
  • Necessity: Required for core functionality
  • Data: Never sent to any server, stored locally only

Accessibility Service Permission

  • Purpose: To detect and block app launches when limits are exceeded
  • Necessity: Required for app blocking functionality
  • Data: Only used in real-time, not stored or transmitted

Overlay Permission

  • Purpose: To display block screens when limits are reached
  • Necessity: Required for blocking interface

VPN Permission

  • Purpose: To block network access for restricted apps
  • Necessity: Optional - only needed for network blocking feature
  • Data: Network traffic is filtered locally, no data is sent to external servers

Notification Permission

  • Purpose: To show usage alerts and limit notifications
  • Necessity: Optional but recommended for alerts

Internet Permission

  • Purpose: To display advertisements only
  • Data: No app functionality data is transmitted; only ad-related requests to Google AdMob

Children's Privacy

Focus does not collect any personal information from users of any age. The app is designed as a productivity and parental control tool. Advertisements shown are served by Google AdMob, which complies with COPPA requirements for child-directed content.

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your locally stored data:

  • Access: View all data directly in the app's interface
  • Modify: Change any limits, settings, or preferences at any time
  • Delete: All local data is immediately deleted when you uninstall the app
  • No Export Needed: Since no data leaves your device, there's nothing to export or download

For advertising data collected by Google AdMob:

  • Manage ad personalization through Google Ad Settings
  • Request data deletion from Google directly
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings

Data Retention

  • All data is stored only on your device while the app is installed
  • Historical usage data is kept locally for 30 days for statistics display
  • Uninstalling the app immediately and permanently deletes all local data
  • We have no data to retain on our servers because we don't collect any

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share any data because we do not collect any:

  • No user data is sent to Focus developers
  • No usage statistics are collected by us
  • No analytics services are used by Focus
  • The only third-party service is Google AdMob for advertising
  • AdMob may collect device and ad interaction data as described above
